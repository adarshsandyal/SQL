# SQL
```sql
-- 1. What is the total amount each customer spent at the restaurant? 
SELECT
  	customer_id, 
    sum(price) as total_price
FROM dannys_diner.sales s 
INNER JOIN dannys_diner.menu m on s.product_id = m.product_id
GROUP BY customer_id
```
